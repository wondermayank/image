Frequently asked questions

Do these filters reduce image quality?

No. Filters only adjust color and tone values; the output keeps the same resolution as your source photo unless you change the export size.

Can I control how strong a filter looks?

Yes. Every filter has an intensity slider so you can blend it anywhere between the original photo and the full effect.

Are my photos uploaded anywhere?

No. All filtering happens locally in your browser using canvas, and your photo never leaves your device.

Which filter is closest to a classic film look?

Vintage and Faded Film both mimic analog color shifts and lifted shadows, while Sepia leans toward an older, warm monochrome tone.
