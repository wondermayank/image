Frequently asked questions

What aspect ratio should a passport photo be?

A standard passport photo uses a 35:45 aspect ratio (roughly 7:9), matching the international 35mm × 45mm passport photo standard.

How do I remove white borders from a scanned signature?

Use the auto-trim feature, which detects the outer rows and columns of near-uniform background color and crops them away automatically, leaving just the signature or photo content.

Can I crop a photo to a custom ratio?

Yes — switch to "Free crop" mode, which lets you drag the crop box's corner handles to any ratio or size you need, instead of being locked to a fixed ratio.

Does cropping resize my photo to specific pixel dimensions?

Cropping only changes the visible area and ratio, not the exact pixel count. If your exam portal also requires a specific pixel size like 200×230px, use the Image Resizer tool after cropping.
