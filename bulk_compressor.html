Frequently Asked Questions

How many images can I compress at once?

There's no hard limit — 50, 100, even 500+ images work. Performance depends on your device's memory. Large batches may take a moment.

Is the compression lossless?

Yes. This tool uses Canvas re-encoding which is visually lossless. For pixel-perfect lossless, use the dedicated Lossless Compress tool.

What formats are supported?

JPEG, PNG, and WebP input files are supported. Output is re-encoded in the same format.

Can I download all compressed images at once?

Yes — click "Download ZIP" to get all compressed images bundled in a single ZIP file.
