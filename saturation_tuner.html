Frequently asked questions

What is saturation in photo editing?

Saturation controls how vivid or muted the colors in a photo appear. High saturation makes colors pop; low saturation moves toward grayscale.

How is hue rotation different from saturation?

Saturation changes color intensity; hue rotation shifts every color around the color wheel, turning reds toward orange or blue, for creative color grading.

Are my photos uploaded anywhere?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using canvas. Your photo never leaves your device.

Can I completely remove color to make it black and white?

Yes. Drag the saturation slider all the way left to 0% for a full grayscale conversion.
