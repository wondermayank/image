Frequently asked questions

Can I use a custom font for my text?

Yes — pick from sans, serif, monospace, script, or bold display styles in the font dropdown. The tool runs entirely on system and web-safe fonts so it works instantly without any uploads.

How do I move the text on the image?

Click or tap directly on the text in the preview and drag it anywhere on the photo. The position updates live so you can fine-tune placement before downloading.

Will the text show up in the downloaded file?

Yes, the text is drawn directly onto the image pixels at full resolution, so it appears exactly as previewed in the final PNG download.

Can I add a shadow behind the text for readability?

Yes, toggle the Shadow style chip to add a soft drop shadow behind your text, which helps it stand out on busy or light-colored backgrounds.
