Frequently asked questions

What is Gaussian blur?

Gaussian blur softens an image by averaging each pixel with its neighbors using a bell-curve weight distribution. It is the most common blur type for creating soft, dreamy backgrounds.

What is tilt-shift blur?

Tilt-shift keeps a horizontal band in sharp focus while blurring the top and bottom, simulating the look of miniature photography or a shallow depth of field.

Are my photos uploaded anywhere?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser using canvas. Your photo never leaves your device.

Does blurring reduce image resolution?

No. The output keeps the same pixel dimensions as your source. Only pixel values are blended; the file size may decrease slightly.
