Frequently asked questions

How many images can I resize at once?

You can queue 100 or more images in a single batch. Everything is processed in your browser, so the only real limit is your device's memory rather than a fixed cap.

Will resizing reduce image quality?

Shrinking an image rarely causes visible quality loss. You can also control the JPEG quality slider to balance file size against sharpness for each batch.

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

No. All resizing happens locally using your browser's canvas, and files never leave your device.

Can I resize by width, height, or both?

Yes. Choose to fit by width, by height, or by the longest side, and the tool keeps each photo's original aspect ratio automatically.
