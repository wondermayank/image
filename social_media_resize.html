Frequently Asked Questions

What size should Instagram posts be?

Instagram recommends 1080×1080 pixels for square posts. For portrait posts, use 1080×1350. Stories should be 1080×1920 (9:16 ratio).

What's the best size for a YouTube thumbnail?

YouTube recommends 1280×720 pixels (16:9 ratio) with a minimum width of 640px. Keep file size under 2MB for best results.

What are the correct dimensions for a Facebook cover photo?

Facebook cover photos display at 820×312 pixels on desktop and 640×360 on mobile. Use 820×312 and keep important content centered for cross-device compatibility.

What size should a Twitter/X header image be?

Twitter recommends 1500×500 pixels for header images. The aspect ratio is 3:1. Keep in mind the bottom portion may be slightly cropped on some devices.

What's the recommended LinkedIn banner size?

LinkedIn recommends 1584×396 pixels for personal profile banners. For company pages, use 1128×191. The profile photo overlaps the bottom-left of the banner, so keep that area clear.
