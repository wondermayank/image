Frequently asked questions

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. The AI model runs entirely inside your browser using WebAssembly. Your image is never sent to any server — processing is fully local to your device.

Why does the first run take longer?

On first use, the AI model files (~40 MB) are downloaded once and cached in your browser. Every subsequent use is instant — and works offline once cached.

What format does the output use?

The tool exports a transparent PNG. The background is replaced with full transparency so you can place the subject on any colour or new background.

What kinds of images work best?

The AI model performs well on portraits, product photos, animals, and objects with clear foreground/background contrast. Very cluttered or low-contrast scenes may produce less accurate masks.
