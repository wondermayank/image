Frequently asked questions

How does the palette extraction work?

The tool samples pixels from your image on a downscaled canvas, then clusters similar colors using a median-cut inspired quantization algorithm to find the most representative colors in the image.

Can I copy the color codes?

Yes. Click any swatch card to copy its hex code. The "Copy All HEX" button copies all extracted colors as a comma-separated list.

How many colors can I extract?

You can extract between 4 and 12 dominant colors from your image. Use the count slider to adjust how many swatches are shown.

Is this tool free and private?

Yes — completely free and private. Everything runs in your browser. Your image is never uploaded anywhere.
